# Amar RPG Mythology

## Creation Story

The universe began with **Uhr**, an all-knowing deity seeking entertainment through creation. Uhr generated **Magick**, representing chaos and mystery. Their interaction produced the foundational elements:

- **Alesia** (earth)
- **Walmaer** (water)
- **Shalissa** (wind)
- **Ikalio** (fire)

**Ielina** watches over the world when Ikalio is resting. She is also the timekeeper, managing existence's cycles.

## Primary Gods (The Six)

The core pantheon includes the four elements' rulers plus Ielina (moon) and the Time Lords (preservers of existence).

### The Four Elements
- **Alesia** (Earth) - Grants earth/protection magick
- **Walmaer** (Water) - God of water and seas
- **Shalissa** (Wind) - Goddess of wind and freedom
- **Ikalio** (Fire) - God of fire and the sun

### Other Primary Gods
- **Ielina** (Moon) - Delivers perception magick, timekeeper
- **The Time Lords** - Preservers of existence

## Secondary Gods

Secondary deities emerged from divine interactions:

- **Anashina** (Nature) - Creator of faeries and natural world
- **Elesi** (Creation) - Goddess of creation
- **Kraagh** (Death/Reincarnation) - God of death and the cycle of rebirth
- **Taroc** - Provides melee combat skills
- **McGillan and Gwendyll** - Exclusively worshipped by nobles; their union established human civilization
- **Juba** - Popular among common folk
- **Tzankili** (Thievery) - God of thieves
- **Man Peggon, Elesi, Mestronorpha, Maleko** - Considered cursed gods
- **Barg** - Dwarven god

### Lesser Deities (Daily Worship)
- **Recolar** (Competition) - Day 1
- **Mailatroz** (Trading) - Day 2
- **Ztenasie** (Food) - Day 3
- **Elaari** (Arts) - Day 4
- **Tsankili** (Thievery) - Day 5
- **Fooradur** (Diplomacy) - Day 6
- **Liandra** (Hope) - Day 7

## Religious Structure

Followers organize into cults per deity with ranked membership:

- **Lay Members**: No special benefits
- **Initiates**: Gain a +3 bonus on their god's holy day
- **Priests**: Receive +3 bonus five days before/after holy days; +6 on the actual day

Each god grants specific powers to their followers.

## Calendar System

The year contains **13 months** (one per major god), each with **four weeks of seven days** (28 days per month, 364 days per year).

Days honor the lesser deities listed above, cycling through the week.

## Historical Timeline

- **-20 ort**: Creation begins
- **-19 ort**: Lesser gods emerge
- **-15 ort**: Great power war erupts between gods
- **-9 ort**: Dragons aid in establishing balance; faeries enter the world
- **Year 0**: Human civilization begins through Macgillan and Gwendyll's union
- **Year 354** (current): The Kingdom of Amar was founded 354 years ago

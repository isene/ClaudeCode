# The Kingdom of Amar

## Kingdom Overview

The Kingdom of Amar, founded **354 years ago**, is "a fairly peaceful kingdom" with "a stable society and its problems are mostly predictable."

- **Total Population**: ~50,000 humans
- **Noble Population**: ~900
- **Year**: 354 (current year since kingdom founding)

The realm is geographically isolated by fog to the east and south, making escape impossible.

## Government Structure

### Royalty
- **King Caolain II**: Walmaer worshipper; big and sturdy, somewhat rude but trying to be elegant
- **Queen Fiona**: Shalissa priestess; very elegant lady and spiritually aware
- **Prince Vaiangor** (age 333): Sports enthusiast; idol figure for healthy living
- **Princess Ianira** (age 331): Artist (singer, painter, poet, flautist); Ielina initiate

### Military
- **Commander Seillan Torthal**: Military leader and Taroc warlord

### Administration
- Five Barons govern districts
- The King directly oversees Amaronir

## Major Religions

Primary faiths include **Walmaer, Shalissa, Anashina, Alesia, Taroc, and Juba**.

Nobles **exclusively** worship **McGillan and Gwendyll**.

**Man Peggon, Elesi, Mestronorpha, and Maleko** are considered cursed.

## Underground Organizations

### The Calah
Rebel movement opposing the King; often Tzankili worshippers.

### Dark Dagger
Assassination guild; rumored Kraagh/Mestronorpha worshippers.

### The Cloaks
Thieves' guild with mandatory membership; mostly Tzankili devotees.

## Magick in the Kingdom

Only **1 in 50 humans** possesses Magical Aptitude (MA).

Among those with MA:
- Only 1/3 develop a Womp (magical focus)
- Only 1/2 of those learn spells

**The Magick Circle** consists of eight powerful wizards led by "**The Rai**," a summoning specialist.

Between **300-400 citizens** practice magick across various paths.

**Black magick is banned** by royal decree.

## Special Locations

### Within the Kingdom

**The Eliminator**
Underground maze beneath royal castle; annual trial where 1 in 10 survive; winners gain nobility.

**The Rift**
Premium adventurer inn; excellent service for regulars; run by warrior Sharina.

**The Magick Cauldron**
Inn frequenting would-be magicians.

**The Challenge Ground**
Annual wizard dueling arena (Spell Magick Day).

**The Round Hill**
Meditation site providing peace and relaxation.

**The Oracle of Varni**
Sacred pond with wise tree spirit; answers yes/no questions.

### Beyond the Kingdom

**The Magick Ring**
Ring of oak trees in faerie woods; offers life-changing interventions.

**The Dead Marsh**
Cursed swamp where necromancer Arachan fought the King; produces zombies from the dead.

**The Dark Woods**
Evil forest between troll and faerie realms; inhabited by corrupted faeries and witches; black magic prevalent.

**The Water Maze**
Island labyrinth with water dragon Aia.

**The Circle's Tower**
Invisible, invulnerable wizard tower; inspires fear.

**The Old Dwarven Caves**
Demon-infested mines; source of lost dwarven treasures.

## District Details

### Amaronir (Capital)
- Population: 18,000
- Ruled by: King Caolain II
- Features:
  - Academica Magicka (wizard academy)
  - The Coliseum (gladiatorial games)
  - Wealthiest district
  - Cultural center

### Rauinir (Resort City)
- Population: 12,000
- Ruled by: Baron Fer Chalun
- Features:
  - "Walmaer capital"
  - Fishing industry
  - Resort destination
  - Coastal location

### Aleresir (Forest District)
- Population: 3,000
- Ruled by: Lady Serena Chiall
- Features:
  - Heavily forested
  - Anashina worship dominant
  - Strictest magick regulations
  - Nature-focused

### Feronir (Strict City)
- Population: 3,500
- Ruled by: Baron Garos Maella
- Features:
  - Strictest city laws
  - Guards have execution authority
  - Law and order focused

### Calaronir (City of Thieves)
- Population: 9,000
- Ruled by: Baronesse Fienna Milin
- Features:
  - "City of Thieves"
  - Magick prohibited within walls
  - Center of underworld activity
  - Home to The Cloaks guild

### Mieronir (Military Hub)
- Population: 7,000
- Ruled by: Alac Geoffryn
- Features:
  - Defense hub
  - Least taxed district
  - Military flotilla base
  - Strategic importance

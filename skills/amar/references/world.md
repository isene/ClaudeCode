# The World of Amar

## Geography

The world of Amar is largely confined by natural barriers:
- **Mountains** dominate the North
- **Forests** cover the West
- **Fog** obscures the Southern and Eastern shores

These barriers limit inhabitants' geographic knowledge beyond the kingdom's borders.

## Climate

The planetary axis tilts at 15 degrees (compared to Earth's 23.4 degrees), resulting in minimal seasonal variation.

The Kingdom of Amar experiences weather comparable to **France's climate**.

## Celestial Features

Citizens observe a distinctive night sky with specific constellations visible from their vantage point.

## Surrounding Peoples

### The Dwarves

Descendants of cursed human warriors, dwarves are sturdy and skilled metalworkers.

**Characteristics:**
- Worship Barg and Alesia
- Intense fear of heights
- Excellent mining and metal-working abilities
- Hard bargainers; never give anything free
- Skeptical of human magic
- Hatred for trolls and Araxi

**Leadership:**
- **Dwarven King Gorm** rules from **Borgheim** capital

### Trolls

Huge, powerful, solitary creatures thriving on hate.

**Organization:**
- Five Grand Chieftains rule territories
- Terrorize surrounding Araxi populations
- Extremely dangerous

### Araxi

Evil forest-dwellers organized in packs.

**Characteristics:**
- Hate everything indiscriminately
- Organized in pack structures
- Frequently terrorized by trolls
- Generally hostile to all other races

### Faeries

Otherworldly beings from a purer realm created by Anashina.

**Types:**
- Elves
- Brownies
- Pixies
- Leprechauns
- Water spirits

**Characteristics:**
- Generally uninterested in human affairs
- Known for practical jokes without regard for consequences
- Love art and mimic human creativity
- From a realm purer than the mortal world

### Elves

Faerie race resembling slender humans.

**Physical Traits:**
- Pointed ears
- Large cat-like eyes
- Slender build

**Characteristics:**
- Wise and spiritual
- Possess innate magic
- Expert archers and hunters
- Anashina's favored race

## Notable Creatures

### Dragons

**Origo**
- Ancient dragon
- Obsessed with treasure and magic
- Lives in northwestern mountains
- Highly dangerous
- Disrespectful to other creatures

**Seven Wyverns**
- Minor dragons in eastern mountains
- Active threats to trolls and Araxi
- Treasure collectors
- Territorial

**Aia**
- Female water dragon
- Inhabits the Water Maze on southern island
- Lazy and prefers solitude
- Less aggressive than other dragons

## Population Knowledge

Residents possess understanding of:
- Seasonal cycles
- Sky observation and navigation
- Local geography

However, their comprehension of broader world geography remains limited by the geographic barriers surrounding them.

## Relations Between Peoples

### Alliances
- Humans and Dwarves: Trade partners, mutual respect
- Elves and Faeries: Natural kinship through Anashina

### Conflicts
- Dwarves vs. Trolls: Ancient hatred
- Dwarves vs. Araxi: Active hostility
- Humans vs. Araxi: Defensive conflicts
- Trolls vs. Everyone: Solitary aggression

### Neutral/Indifferent
- Faeries and Humans: Minimal interaction, occasional pranks
- Dragons and most races: Dragons generally ignore others unless provoked or treasure is involved
